Final Concept Overview

Brush Buddy is a handheld toothbrush with an integrated speaker and timer. A single button starts a 2-minute song; when the music ends, brushing is complete. The device can connect to a parent app for notifications. The form factor prioritizes a kid-friendly grip and water resistance.
