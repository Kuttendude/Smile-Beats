At a Glance

From problem to solution in one simple flow

The Problem

Kids often skip or rush through brushing. Dentists recommend 2 full minutes, but most children brush for 30 seconds or less, leading to cavities and poor oral health habits.

Our Solution

Brush Buddy combines music with a built-in 2-minute timer. Kids brush along to a song — when the music stops, they're done. Parents get peace of mind through connected notifications.

Why It Works

Music turns brushing from a chore into an activity. The timer removes guesswork, and kids build positive associations with oral care that last a lifetime.

Abstract

Smile Beats presents Brush Buddy, a music-powered toothbrush designed to help kids brush for the full two minutes dentists recommend. The product addresses a common challenge: children often rush or skip brushing, leading to poor oral health. Brush Buddy plays a 2-minute song while kids brush, turning a daily chore into an engaging routine. Built for ages 3 and up, it features a kid-friendly grip, soft bristles, and optional parent notifications. This portfolio documents our research, ideation, design, testing, and reflection throughout the Capstone project. Our goal is to make brushing fun — two minutes at a time, one big smile at a time.
