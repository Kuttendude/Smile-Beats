Brainstorming

We explored multiple directions for addressing kids' brushing challenges. Here are some of the ideas we considered during our brainstorming sessions.

Music timer toothbrush

Toothbrush with built-in speaker that plays a 2-minute song.

App-based timer

Phone or tablet app that plays music and times brushing.

Reward stickers

Physical sticker chart to track and reward brushing.

Smart mirror display

Bathroom mirror that shows a timer and fun animations.

Concepts Considered

We narrowed our brainstorm to concepts that were feasible, kid-friendly, and directly addressed the timing and engagement problem. We evaluated each for manufacturability, cost, and impact.

Why Brush Buddy Won

Brush Buddy emerged as the strongest concept because it combines music and timing into a single, kid-held device. No phone or screen required — kids can use it independently. The 2-minute song makes the time tangible and enjoyable. Parent connectivity adds accountability. It's simple, scalable, and directly solves the problem.
